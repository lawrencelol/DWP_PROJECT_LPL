Children of The Star

Price: RM 20.00

Author: Stella Drew

Publisher: Star Publisher

Synopsis:

        Star light, star bright;
        Like a diamond in the sky
        I wish I may, I wish I might;
        Dear children of star please don't cry.

        Legend says that every 1000 years, hundreds of purple light will shoot across Earth. That's not shooting stars, but the gradiant spirits of the children of the star, searching for the chosen one to guide them back to their stars, to their HOME.
        "Wait. Purple shooting stars? Well that's new... they are coming towards me!!!" said Lyra.
