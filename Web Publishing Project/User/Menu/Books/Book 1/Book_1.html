Flippy The Silly Little Fish

Price: RM 12.00

Author: Noah Grantt

Publisher: Dream Publisher

Synopsis:

        Flippy is a silly little fish that got separated from his family due to a strong undercurrent. To reunite with his family, he embarked on a solo journey. Along the way, he encountered various ocean residents, including a shark who loves planting corals, an evil clownfish, and even a granny octopus who enjoys collecting seashells.
        Flippy's story will teach children not to judge others by their appearance and the importance to take the first step.
