ABOUT US

Welcome to LPL (Look-Pick-Learn) Bookstore, the premier destination for a literary universe at your fingertips. Established in 2024, LPL Bookstore is committed to help readers of all ages finding the books they love and the narratives that inspire them.

OUR TEAM

MISSION AND VISION

Mission

At LPL Bookstore, our mission is to ignite passion for reading by providing an extensive selection of eBooks that are easily accessible and affordable for everyone. We aim to foster a community of readers and writers by offering a platform that supports the sharing of literary creations. Our goal is to enhance reading experience through exceptional customer service.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading online destination for book lovers, where every reader can find their next favorite book and every author can find their audience. By continually expanding our collection, improving our platform, and supporting the literary community, we aspire to inspire and connect readers and writers across the globe.

OUR COMMITMENT

Quality Content We carefully curate our selection to ensure that every book we offer meets our standards of quality and excellence.
Author Support We are proud to support both established and emerging authors. Our platform provides a space for writers to share their stories with a global audience.
Customer Satisfaction Your satisfaction is our top priority. Our customer service team is here to help with any questions or issues you may have.

EVENT

"Burst Out Your Mind" Digital Novel-Writing Competition

At here, we are passionate about fostering people's creativity. On February 2024, we hosted our very first "Burst Out Your Mind" Digital Novel-Writing Competition. The response was overwhelming, with hundreds of participants submitting their original works.

The winning novels "My Mind is A Mess" were featured prominently in our online bookstore, offering the authors a unique opportunity to reach a wider audience.

Stay tuned for future events and competitions as we continue to support and celebrate the literary community.

COMMUNITY AND BEYOND

We believe in the collective power of community. We take great pride in providing writers, both developing and established, with a voice and a platform. Join us at LPL Bookstore and be a part of our active reader community. We are here to encourage your love of books at every stage, whether you're interested in a captivating mystery, delving into an intriguing biography, or losing yourself in a world of fantasy.

We appreciate you choosing LPL Bookstore. Enjoy your reading!
